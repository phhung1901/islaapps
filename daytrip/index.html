Isla Apps: Day Tripper's Guide

Designed specifically for visitors who come to Isla Mujeres for the day. This travel guide not only covers practical and useful information such as basic facts, how to get to Isla, how to get around, what to see and what to do, but it goes much, much further. With loads of information about Isla's rich history, culture and traditions, interesting tidbits about island life and the stories and legends of the days when pirates walked the shores.
